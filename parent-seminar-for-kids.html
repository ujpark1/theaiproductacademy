And Why Start at Age 8?

AI Product Building is the skill of discovering real problems, imagining solutions, building apps, and sharing them with real people. It's about turning curiosity into creation.

At the heart of this process is Design Thinking — a creative, human-centered approach used by the world's best inventors and product teams. And it's something kids are naturally good at.

Fun fact: Uijun's Amazon bestselling book is titled “Design Thinking.” He's spent 15+ years mastering this process — and now he's making it accessible to kids.

AI can write code and generate images. But it can't decide what to build or why it matters. That's where your child's imagination comes in — and that's the skill that will matter most.

Real Story

What Happens When a Kid
Learns to Build with AI

A 10-year-old noticed that her family always argued about whose turn it was to do chores. Instead of just complaining, she decided to fix it.

Using design thinking, she talked to her family members about what frustrated them. She researched chore apps — and realized none of them were fun enough for kids to actually want to use.

She built a family chore app where completing tasks earns stars and unlocks fun rewards. Using AI tools, she went from idea to a working app her whole family now uses every week.

No coding classes. No computer science background. Just curiosity, a structured process, and AI tools that made building possible. This is what happens when kids learn to think like creators.
