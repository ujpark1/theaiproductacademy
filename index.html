Our Mission

Why We Exist

The world is changing faster than schools can keep up. AI is transforming every industry, yet most teens are only taught to use technology, not to create with it. We believe every young person deserves the skills to go from idea to product — the same process used by the best teams in tech.

Making something is different from making it well

Anyone can prompt an AI to generate code. But building a product people actually want requires empathy, critical thinking, and a structured process. That's what we teach.

AI literacy is the new literacy

Reading, writing, math — and now AI. Teens who learn to work with AI as a creative tool will have an unfair advantage in any career they choose.

Schools teach answers. We teach questions.

Standardized education rewards memorization. But the most valuable skill in the AI era is knowing which problems are worth solving — and how to solve them.

It started with my own daughter

I watched my teenage daughter identify a real problem her friends had — forgetting homework and procrastinating — and build an actual app to solve it using AI. No coding classes. No bootcamp. Just a clear process and the right tools. That moment changed everything for me.

I realized schools can't teach this

Schools are designed for a world that no longer exists. They teach subjects in isolation, reward right answers over good questions, and have no framework for building real things. Our kids deserve better preparation for the world they'll actually enter.

So I built what I wished existed

AI Product Academy combines the professional product development process I've used for 15 years with AI tools that make building accessible to anyone. It's the education I wish every teen had access to — starting with my own kids.

Our Approach

Design Thinking + AI = Real Products

We teach the same 5-step process used by professional product teams — adapted for teens and powered by AI.

1

Empathy & User Understanding

Interview real people. Understand their frustrations, habits, and unmet needs. Learn to see the world through someone else's eyes.

2

Problem Definition

Research existing solutions. Find the gap. Define a specific problem worth solving — not just any problem, the right one.

3

Ideation & Brainstorming

Generate multiple solutions. Evaluate trade-offs. Pick the approach that best serves your users with the resources you have.

4

Build with AI

Use AI tools to go from idea to working prototype. No coding experience needed — just clear thinking and the right prompts.

5

Test & Present

Put your product in front of real users. Gather feedback. Iterate. Then present your work — because shipping is a skill too.
